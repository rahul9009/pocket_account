Data Deletion

Pocket Account by R&DC Software • Last updated: April 3, 2026

Pocket Account is an offline-first app. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any user data on our servers.

How to Delete Your Data

You can permanently delete all your data from within the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Pocket Account app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap "Clear All Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This action is immediate and irreversible. No data can be recovered after deletion unless you have a backup file.

What Gets Deleted

Data Type Status
All transactions (payments, receipts) Deleted
All party records (creditors, debtors) Deleted
All payment accounts (cash, bank) Deleted
All financial year data Deleted
App settings and preferences Deleted
Google Drive backup files (if enabled) Delete manually from Google Drive
Email backup files (if enabled) Delete manually from your email

Alternative: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Pocket Account from your device will also remove all locally stored data, including the database, settings, and cached files.

Data We Do Not Store

Since Pocket Account does not collect or store any data on remote servers, there is no server-side data to delete. Your data exists only on your device.
